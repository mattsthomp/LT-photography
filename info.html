heart and eye along the same line of sight."

Photography for me is a form of artistic expression. Through my photos I can express my love and admiration for the family dynamic; those special moments that really show the bond between siblings, parents and their children and the love shared within the family. Capturing these special moments in time is my ultimate goal and the reason I chose to begin my business so I can share my photos with others.
